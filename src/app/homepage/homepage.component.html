About Pusat Tuisyen Pintar Cahaya

Mr. Steven Yap, founder of PTPC has an ambitious vision to lead PTPC to be the Top Education Coaching in Malaysia. Pusat Tuisyen Pintar Cahaya (PTPC) is located in the heart of Ampang and has been delivering high quality tuition classes and short courses since 2004. The overall aim of the tuition centre is to ensure that through high quality teaching & learning and personal coaching- students gain the qualifications, broad knowledge, skills and experience to allow them to participate in a competitive global economy.

About Tutor Go

Tutor Go is a web application that targets to assist student to find suitable tutor or course, to give tutor the opportunity to broaden their reach for parents/students and to help tuition center to manage their clients.
